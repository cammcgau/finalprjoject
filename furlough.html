Some words about furlough in the UK:

Are you unsure what 'furlough' means? Some facts about it:
  • Furlough is a temporary leave of employees due to special needs of a company.
  • During the peak of the coronavirus pandemic, the government paid 80% of furloughed employee wages.
  • It covered the wages of 9.6 million wages and in May at the peak over 30% of the UK workforce was furloughed.
  • Over £50bn was spent on furlough from March to September.
  • The government has recently extended the scheme to March 2021.
  • Figures show that over half of furloughed workers returned to work.
